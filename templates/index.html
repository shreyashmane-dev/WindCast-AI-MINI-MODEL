Operations

Why Wind Forecasting Matters

01

Grid Load Balancing

Grid operators must match supply and demand in real time. Accurate wind forecasts help prevent local power grid imbalances or sudden power drop-off shortages.

02

Operational Blade Safety

Establishes warning systems when wind speeds exceed safety ratings (18+ m/s cutout speed), preventing turbine generator overload or mechanical blade fatigue.

03

Optimal Maintenance Scheduling

Forecasts low-wind windows programmatically, allowing operators to plan turbine maintenance downtimes without incurring massive grid standby costs.
